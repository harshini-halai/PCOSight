PCOS Awareness & Healthy Lifestyle

PCOS affects both physical and emotional health. Awareness and healthy habits play an important role in symptom management.

Healthy Lifestyle Recommendations

  • Maintain regular exercise routines
  • Follow balanced nutrition
  • Reduce stress through mindfulness activities
  • Track menstrual cycles regularly
  • Sleep for 7-8 hours daily
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day

Dietary Tips

  • Consume high-fiber foods
  • Reduce excessive sugar intake
  • Include fruits and vegetables in meals
  • Prefer healthy protein sources
  • Avoid highly processed foods

When to Consult a Doctor?

If symptoms such as irregular periods, sudden weight gain, excessive hair growth, or hormonal imbalance continue for long durations, professional medical guidance is recommended.

Healthy Diet

Include fiber-rich foods, fruits, vegetables, and healthy proteins.

Exercise

Regular physical activity helps maintain hormonal balance and healthy weight.

Stress Management

Meditation and quality sleep help reduce stress-related hormonal imbalance.

Medical Consultation

Regular checkups and professional guidance are important for symptom management.
