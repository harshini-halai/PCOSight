What is PCOS?

PCOS stands for Polycystic Ovary Syndrome, a hormonal disorder affecting women's reproductive health.

Common Symptoms

  • Irregular periods
  • Weight gain
  • Hair growth
  • Acne

Why Awareness Matters?

Early detection and healthy lifestyle management can help reduce complications.
